About Us

Ora-Talk Dental Initiative Uganda is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving oral health awareness and providing dental care accessibility to underserved communities across Uganda. We believe that a healthy smile is a fundamental right.


Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for a cavity-free Uganda where every citizen values and has access to quality oral healthcare.

Meet Our Founders

Founded in 2022, Ora-Talk Dental Initiative Uganda began as a vision shared by three dedicated dental students. While witnessing the gaps in oral healthcare during their training, they decided to take action, transforming their academic passion into a community-driven movement.

Oral Health Tips

The 2x2 Rule

Brush your teeth for at least 2 minutes, twice a day (morning and before bed).

Watch the Sugar

Limit sugary snacks and sodas. Choose water and crunchy fruits instead.

Change your Brush

Replace your toothbrush every 3 months or after you have been sick.

Aims & Objectives

Awareness

To educate schools and rural communities on proper dental hygiene practices.

Outreach

To organize free dental camps providing checkups, cleanings, and basic extractions.

Partnerships

To collaborate with global health bodies to bring modern dental tech to Uganda.

Advocacy

To lobby for better oral health policies within the national healthcare system.
